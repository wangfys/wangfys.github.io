EXPERIENCE

  • Microsoft Research Asia, May 2020 - September 2020
    • Research and Development Intern at Internet Graphics group
    • I participated in creating low-cost indoor image labeling datasets. First, I worked on an RGBD SLAM program using KinectV1 and GPUs to reconstruct indoor scenes at runtime. This aims at estimating the performance of existing real-time SLAM algorithms and exploring the possibility of using an iPhone with LiDAR to collect 3D data at a low cost. Second, I worked on rendering a prelabeled 3D indoor dataset to generate image segmentation data for special use. The rendered images are evaluated by training an HRNet (proposed by icrosoft) and compared with training curves on real image datasets.
  • Microsoft Research Asia, Feburary 2019 - June 2019
    • Research and Development Intern at Internet Graphics group
    • I participated in the migration of O-CNN and Adaptive O-CNN from Caffe to TensorFlow and finished my undergraduate thesis. O-CNN and Adaptive O-CNN are Octree-based 3D convolutional networks proposed by Internet Graphics group before. I migrated all the important operators from Caffe to TensorFlow and some preprocessing operators from CPU to GPU. In the last two weeks of my internship, I gained experience in capturing and processing depth images using depth sensors such as Kinect.
  • Cambricon Technology, July 2018 – August 2018
    • SDK Development Intern at MXNet team
    • I participated in C++ Development of the adaptation of MXNet framework for Cambricon Machine Learning Unit and worked on the inference of SSD and Faster-RCNN network under several modes(single-core/multi-core, dense parameters/sparse parameters) on both Python and C++ level.
